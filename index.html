About Us

Leading Initiative for Education (LIFE) is a vibrant community-based organization (CBO) established in October 2023 by KeMUST Mental Health Advocates in collaboration with YES Alumni. LIFE is devoted to promoting mental health awareness, leadership development, educational advancement, and youth empowerment. The organization operates through strategic partnerships and collaborative efforts, striving to create a positive and lasting impact in these critical areas.

LIFE was born out of the successful implementation of a mental health project conducted at Meru University and Kenya Methodist University in October 2023. Recognizing the need to build on this momentum, LIFE was established to leverage the dedication, skills, and passion of its members in addressing broader community challenges, specifically around education, leadership, and literacy. The organization aims to nurture the potential of young people and equip them with the tools to drive meaningful change in their communities.

At its core, LIFE believes that empowered and well-informed individuals are the cornerstone of thriving societies. By offering a platform for youth to grow, learn, and lead, the organization hopes to cultivate a generation of responsible, self-aware leaders. Through its work, LIFE seeks to break down barriers to education and mental health services, and to provide resources that uplift individuals and communities alike. With its focus on collaboration, LIFE ensures that no one works alone in the mission to create healthier, more educated, and empowered communities.
